Academics at Hosanna Secondary School

School Curriculum

Hosanna Secondary School follows the national curriculum which includes a broad range of subjects in Sciences, Humanities, and Arts to prepare students for Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).

Departments

  • Science Department (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Mathematics Department
  • Languages Department (English, Kiswahili, French)
  • Humanities Department (History, Geography, Religious Education)
  • Technical Department (Computer Studies, Agriculture, Commerce)

Co-Curricular Activities

We encourage students to participate in sports, drama, music, debate, and community service to develop leadership, teamwork, and communication skills.

Academic Calendar

The academic year is divided into three terms: First Term (February - April), Second Term (May - August), and Third Term (September - November). Exams and school events are scheduled accordingly.
