Admissions Guidelines

Enrollment Procedures

To enroll at Hosanna Secondary School, prospective students must complete an application form and submit certified copies of their previous academic records along with a birth certificate. Applications are accepted during the annual admission period.

Fee Structure

  • Tuition Fee: UGX 600,000 per term
  • Registration Fee: UGX 50,000 (one-time)
  • Examination Fee: UGX 40,000 per term
  • Accommodation Fee (if applicable): UGX 150,000 per term
